About Air Tech Aviation School

Our Business Goals

  • Create a safe and supportive environment for young girls in aviation
  • Offer flexible learning modes (Full-time, Part-time, International students)
  • Increase diversity of men and women in the aviation industry
  • Provide affordable, high-quality training
  • Expand courses and enroll both young females and males
  • Achieve over 500 students per semester with 70–100% pass rate
  • Host educational summits, hackathons, and networking events

Our Objective

To empower young girls and women to pursue careers in aviation, break down gender barriers, and build a more diverse and inclusive industry.

Target Audience

Primary Focus: Young female aviators (75–90% of target market)

  • Aspiring Pilots – Ages 16-21, recent school leavers
  • Career Changers – Ages 22-35, women in corporate jobs
  • Tech Specialists – Ages 18-45, interested in drones

Geographic reach: Botswana (especially Maun) and SADC region.

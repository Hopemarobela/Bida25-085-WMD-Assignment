Our Aviation Programs

Flexible, affordable, and inspiring training designed especially for women and young girls.

Private Pilot License (PPL)

Start your flying journey with our beginner-friendly pilot training.

Comprehensive ground school and 40+ hours of flight training. Ideal for aspiring pilots aged 16-21. Includes theory, simulator sessions, and actual flight time in Maun’s busy airspace.

Drone Pilot Certification

Master commercial drone operations and aerial technology.

Perfect for tech enthusiasts. Covers drone regulations in Botswana, flight planning, photography, mapping, and safety. Short course with high demand in tourism and agriculture sectors.

Cabin Crew & Ground Handling

Develop essential skills for airline cabin crew and airport operations.

Focus on safety procedures, customer service, emergency handling, and aviation English. Great entry point for career changers and those preferring non-pilot roles.

Advanced & Commercial Pilot Training

Take your skills to the next level with instrument and commercial ratings.

For students who have completed PPL. Includes night flying, instrument flying, and preparation for commercial pilot license. Flexible part-time options available.
